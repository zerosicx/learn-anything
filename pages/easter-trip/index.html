Easter road trip — Good Friday

Wentworth Point → The Entrance, NSW · 3 April 2026

Good Friday heads up

Cafés are exempt from NSW restricted trading laws, but call ahead to confirm hours. Expect around a 10–15% public holiday surcharge in many places. Both lookouts are free to visit and generally accessible whenever the roads are open; West Head is inside Ku-ring-gai Chase NP — allow $12 per vehicle park entry.
